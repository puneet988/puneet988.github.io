Understanding Climate Model Evaluation and Validation

Abstract

Talk about climate model evaluation

Date
Oct 15, 2019 5:00 PM
Event
ASL 761 - Class presentation
Location
CAS, IIT Delhi


After spending a considerable time pursuing Ph.D. it felt like kind of stagnating with the daily routine. And many times it happens that you often feel like you should have taken a particular course taught by some faculty whose approach and teaching methodology appeals to you at a deeper level. So, I took this course on Earth System Modeling taught by Prof. Sandeep Sahany at my centre and in this course I had an opportunity of giving a talk on climate model evaluation which of course is strongly relevant to my Ph.D. too. Here I am sharing the slides of the talk which I gave. Also I was excited because it was the first time I used $\rm \LaTeX$ beamer template for making the slides which was really fun. 😀

Feedbacks are welcome. 👍
